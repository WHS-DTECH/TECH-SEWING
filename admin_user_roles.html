How It Works

  • Primary Role: Users automatically get their primary role from their Google login email domain and mapping rules.
  • Additional Roles: You can assign extra roles for special permissions without changing the base role.
  • Combined Access: Users can access all routes granted by any of their assigned roles.
  • Email Notification: When you hook up Gmail API, send a "role changed" email to the user and admin team.
